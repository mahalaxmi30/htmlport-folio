About Smriti Mandana


Smriti Mandhana is a famous Indian women’s cricketer. She is a left-handed opening batter and is known for her stylish batting. Smriti Mandhana was born on 18 July 1996 in Mumbai, India. She plays for the Indian women’s cricket team and has won many matches for the country. She has received several awards for her excellent performance in cricket. Smriti Mandhana is an inspiration to young girls who want to become cricketers.
